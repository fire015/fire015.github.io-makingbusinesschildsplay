What others have said

Fuzz Ahmed, National Event Coach High Jump, UKA

Adam Margolin's Making Business Child's Play was a pleasure to read.

As a soon to be Dad I was fascinated with the insights he has learned since the birth of his children Jolie and Fraser.

Juxtaposing the preparation he made for their arrival, to the chaos most of us have in our everyday work life was a humorous start that made me want to read more.

Cunning chapter titles allow the reader to pick up where they left off, as they are clear markers of what is contained in each chapter. They also have the benefit of allowing, at any future date, the reader to pick up on any given point and reference the ideas contained within the chapters with ease.

As an ex-athlete who retrained as an actor, Adam's book is full of excellent skills that allow the expansion and freeing of one's mind. I have often been told I think like a child, after reading this book I will take that as a compliment!

Currently working as UK Athletics National High Jump coach in preparing athletes for 2012, I was very interested in the chapters that gave insight into, 'Improving our attention,' and 'Dealing with fear and change.'

This is a book full of surprises and insights. It's a must read whether you work for a multi national or are dreaming of starting your own business.
